Section 02

Problem & Solution

Understanding the market opportunity and how MyLeadX addresses critical pain points for Indian SMBs.

The Problem

  • High Sales Costs: Indian SMBs spend $15B annually on manual telecalling with poor conversion rates
  • Slow Response Times: Average lead follow-up takes 48+ hours, resulting in 60% lead loss
  • Expensive AI Services: Third-party AI APIs cost $0.50-2.00 per 1000 calls, unaffordable for SMBs
  • Language Barriers: 90% of AI solutions are English-only, ignoring 500M+ regional language speakers
  • Fragmented Tools: Businesses juggle 5+ tools for calls, SMS, WhatsApp, and email

Our Solution

  • AI Voice Agents: Automated outbound/inbound calls with human-like conversations in 10+ languages
  • Self-Hosted AI: 74% cost reduction using Qwen, Whisper, and XTTS models on AWS GPU
  • Unified Platform: Single dashboard for Voice, WhatsApp, SMS, and Email automation
  • Indian Language Support: Native Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, and more
  • SMB-Friendly Pricing: Starting at $49/month vs $500+ for competitors
Market Opportunity
The Indian CRM and sales automation market is projected to reach $3.5 billion by 2027, with SMBs representing 70% of potential customers. MyLeadX is uniquely positioned to capture this market with AI-first, vernacular-language capabilities that no competitor currently offers.
