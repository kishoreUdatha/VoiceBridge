5.1 Your Responsibilities

  • Obtain proper consent before recording any call where required by law

  • Comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws

5.2 Recording Consent

Many jurisdictions require "two-party consent" or "all-party consent" for call recording. This means ALL parties on the call must agree to be recorded. Some jurisdictions only require "one-party consent." You must:
