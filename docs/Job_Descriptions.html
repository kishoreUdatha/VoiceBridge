HR Role

8. HR Executive

Department:Human Resources
Experience:0-1 year
Location:Hyderabad
Salary:₹15,000 - ₹25,000/month

Job Description:

Handle end-to-end HR operations including recruitment, onboarding, and employee engagement for the growing team.

Responsibilities:

Recruitment & Hiring:

  • Post job openings on job portals (Naukri, LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
  • Screen resumes and shortlist candidates
  • Schedule interviews and coordinate with hiring managers
  • Conduct initial HR screening calls
  • Follow up with candidates and collect documents
  • Maintain candidate database and recruitment tracker

Onboarding & Documentation:

  • Prepare offer letters and appointment letters
  • Complete joining formalities for new employees
  • Collect and verify employee documents
  • Create employee IDs and system access
  • Conduct new employee orientation
  • Maintain employee files and records

Attendance & Payroll Support:

  • Track daily attendance and leaves
  • Maintain leave records and balance
  • Assist in monthly payroll processing
  • Handle attendance queries from employees

Employee Engagement:

  • Organize team activities and celebrations
  • Handle employee queries and grievances
  • Assist in performance review process
  • Conduct exit interviews
  • Maintain positive work environment

HR Administration:

  • Maintain HR policies and documents
  • Prepare HR reports and MIS
  • Handle employee communication
  • Coordinate with accounts for salary processing
  • Manage HR software/HRMS

Requirements:

  • 0-1 year experience in HR/recruitment
  • MBA/BBA in HR or related field preferred
  • Good communication skills (English + Hindi)
  • Proficiency in MS Office/Google Workspace
  • Knowledge of job portals (Naukri, LinkedIn)
  • Basic knowledge of labor laws is a plus
  • Organized and detail-oriented
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality
  • Friendly and approachable personality

Good to Have:

  • Experience with HRMS software
  • Knowledge of payroll processing
  • Certification in HR
